ICT (Information and Communications Technology)

The ICT strand focuses on equipping students with the technical skills necessary for various ICT-related careers. It covers topics such as programming, web design, animation, and networking, preparing students for careers in the digital and tech industries.

EIM (Electrical Installation and Maintenance)

The EIM strand trains students in skills related to electrical installation, maintenance, and repair. This strand is ideal for those who want to pursue careers in electrical engineering, technical maintenance, or other hands-on professions in the electrical industry.
