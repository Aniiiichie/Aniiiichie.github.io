STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics)

The STEM strand prepares students for careers in the fields of science, technology, engineering, and mathematics. It emphasizes critical thinking, problem-solving, and innovation, providing students with the foundation to pursue technical and scientific disciplines in the future.

HUMSS (Humanities and Social Sciences)

The HUMSS strand focuses on the study of human behavior, culture, and society. It is ideal for students who wish to pursue careers in education, journalism, law, social work, and other social science-related fields. This strand encourages creativity and critical analysis.

GAS (General Academic Strand)

The GAS strand is designed for students who are still exploring their career paths. It offers a balanced curriculum that includes a mix of subjects from the other strands, allowing students to discover their interests and potential career paths without committing to a specific field.
