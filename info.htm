About Our School

Stand-alone SHS is a school situated in a new site separate from a JHS or Integrated School (IS) and has its own School Head. The Stand Alone Senior High School that is located in Taguig Integrated School (TIS) started in the year of 2016.

SASHS-TIS is one of the partnership-building schools. Its primary goal is to improve the capability of the DepEd and public schools to implement the K to 12 Program. Through partnership-building, the DepEd hopes that the partners will be able to provide the DepEd schools work immersion opportunities, hands-on expertise, additional resources, and livelihood opportunities for senior high school graduates. While partnerships are meant to aid the school in developing students.
